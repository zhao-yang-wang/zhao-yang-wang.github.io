I'm a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Rama Chellappa and Prof. Alan L. Yuille. My research seeks to bridge the gap between artificial and natural intelligence by building scalable structured world models that enable next-generation reasoning and interaction, with applications spanning computer vision, robotics, physical science, and healthcare.
